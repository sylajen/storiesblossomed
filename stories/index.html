Frequently Asked Questions

Do you travel for events?

Absolutely! We love capturing stories wherever they unfold. Whether it's across the country or around the world, we're ready to document your celebration. Travel fees may apply for destination events.

Where are you based?

We're based in the Greater Toronto Area, but we work with clients across Ontario and beyond. Distance is never a barrier to capturing your perfect day.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season. However, we're happy to accommodate shorter timelines when our schedule allows.

When will we receive our photos and videos?

You'll receive a sneak peek within 1-2 weeks. Full galleries are typically delivered within 6-8 weeks, and films within 8-12 weeks. We take time to perfect every detail because your story deserves nothing less.

What's included in your packages?

Each package is customized to your needs, but typically includes full-day coverage, professionally edited photos and/or cinematic films, and an online gallery. We'll work together to create the perfect collection for you.

Can we meet before booking?

We'd love that! We offer complimentary consultations—either virtually or in person—to get to know each other, discuss your vision, and ensure we're the perfect fit for your celebration.
